Pancakes

  • Time: 20
  • Serves: 6 people
Ingredients Quantity
Egg x1
pancake mix 3 cups
milk 2 cups
sugar 3 tablespoons

Step 1: put your pancake mix, eggs, milk, and sugar into a bowl.

Step 2: mix well

Step 3: leave your mixture alone for 5 minutes

Step 4: pour 1/4 a cup of mixture onto a pan on a hot stove and wait until holes form, then flip the pancake

Step 5:leave on other side for thirty seconds to a minute

Step 6:put it on a plate and repeat steps 4-6 until you run out of batter

Pizza

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: 8 people
Ingredients Quantity
pizza dough x1
tomato sauce 3 cups
cheese 3 cups
toppings (optional) NA

Step 1: knead your dough into a circle

Step 2: spread your tomato sauce on the dough

Step 3: add cheese to your pizza

Step 4: add additional toppings

Step 5:bake in oven for 30 minutes

Step 6:cut into 8 pieces and enjoy!

Garlic Bread

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: 8 people
Ingredients Quantity
bread 1 loaf
garlic butter 3/4 cup
cheese 3 cups

Step 1: lay your pieces of bread on a baking sheet

Step 2: spread your garlic butter onto the bread

Step 3: add cheese to your garlic bread

Step 4: put the bread in the oven for 3-5 minutes

Step 5: bring it out and let it sit for 3 minutes

Step 6:enjoy!
